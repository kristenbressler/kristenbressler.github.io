    • About the Mission:

    What is the purpose of this mission?

    We plan to send squirrels to Mars so that they can begin to process of colonization.

    Why send squirrels and not people?

    I may not be particularly fond of squirrels.

    Does the mission have an official theme song?

    Yes, thank you for asking.

    What squirrels are you sending to Mars?

    You can see the entire list of the crew on our crew page. We are currently planning on sending six squirrels to Mars: Neil Acronstrong, Fuzz Aldrin, Charles "Chip" Duke, Scuri Gagarin, Fly Gardner, and Squirrely Ride. But we are always looking for more squirrels to send on our mission. If you know of a squirrel that can be sent on our mission, nominate them on our nomination page.
